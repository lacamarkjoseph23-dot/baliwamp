The Team

The people behind GATAW.

We are Computer Engineering students who developed GATAW as our capstone project. Our goal is to provide an accessible and reliable water quality monitoring system to help Bangus farmers reduce preventable fish kill incidents through real-time data and practical engineering solutions.

B1

Buoy Design & Assembly

Co-designed and assembled the buoy structure, ensuring proper enclosure, component placement, and overall structural stability for field deployment.

B2

Web System Development

Designed and implemented the web-based monitoring platform, integrating real-time data visualization and a threshold-based alert notification system.

B3

CAD Design & Technical Drawings

Created the AutoCAD models and detailed technical drawings of the buoy structure and layout for fabrication.

B4

Hardware & Circuit Design

Designed the circuit schematic, integrated sensors and microcontrollers, and conducted system testing and troubleshooting to ensure stable performance.

B5

Hardware Integration & Buoy Design

Co-designed the buoy structure and assisted in hardware integration, schematic refinement, conducted system testing and troubleshooting to ensure stable performance.

B6

Adviser

Provided academic supervision, technical guidance, and research direction throughout the design, implementation, and validation of the system.
